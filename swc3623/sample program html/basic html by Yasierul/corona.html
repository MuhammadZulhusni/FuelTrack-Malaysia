Overview of Corona Virus

 

 

Corona viruses are a family of viruses that can cause illnesses such as the common cold,severe acute respiratory syndrome (SARS) and Middle East respiratory syndrome (MERS). In 2019, a new corona virus was identified as the cause of disease outbreak that originated in China.

The virus is now known as the severe acute respiratory syndrome corona virus 2 (SARS-Cov-2). The disease it causes is called corona virus disease 2019 (COVID-19). In March 2020, the World Health Organization (WHO) declared the COVID-19 outbreak a pandemic.

Public health groups, including the U.S. Centers for Disease Control and Prevention (CDC) and WHO, are monitoring the pandemic and posting updates on their website. These groups have also issued recommendations for preventing and treating the illness.
